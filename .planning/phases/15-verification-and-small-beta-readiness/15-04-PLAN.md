# 15-04 Plan - TestFlight Readiness Notes

## Goal

Identify what is locally ready, what a reviewer or beta tester should do, and what still blocks a small TestFlight beta.

## Topics

- Reviewer path.
- Demo server and media requirements.
- Privacy and diagnostics posture.
- Asset and metadata readiness.
- Deferred features.
- Blocking items before beta distribution.

## Acceptance

The notes must avoid secrets and make physical hardware proof, signing/provisioning, demo credentials, privacy answers, and review assets explicit blockers.
